What is it ?

The core goal of Gaia is to create a modular platform for chaining tasks together, aggregating outputs, and building useful, actionable information from small expert geospatial models. Think of Gaia like a decoupled "mixture of experts" model - not a singular model per se, but a framework for combining the outputs of many expert models into defined, useful tasks.

Want to read more about our vision in detail? Take a look at the whitepaper.
